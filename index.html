    How It Works

    Our decision engine balances three key metrics to give you an objective "Integrity Score".

    1. Define Options

    Enter the two choices you are debating. Give them clear names.

    2. Rate Metrics

    Rate each option from 1-10 on:

    • Value: Long-term benefit.
    • Joy: Immediate happiness.
    • Effort: Work required.

    3. The Logic

    We calculate an Integrity Score: (Value × 2) + Joy - Effort. Logic beats emotion!

    4. The Verdict

    We analyze the scores and give you a rational justification for the winner.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How accurate is the decision helper?

    It uses a weighted scoring algorithm based on your inputs. While it provides a logical recommendation, the final choice is always yours.

    Is my data saved?

    No, all decision data is processed locally in your browser. We do not store your personal choices.

    Can I add more than two options?

    Currently, the helper is optimized for binary choices (A vs B) to reduce analysis paralysis. Multi-option support is coming in v2.0.

    Is this service really free?

    Yes! The core DecisionHelper tool is 100% free to use for personal decision making.
